About Tourism Toronto

The Toronto Convention & Visitors Association


"Tourism Toronto (a.k.a. " Toronto Convention and Visitors Association, or TCVA ") Toronto’s new online travel company was founded in the year 2018 by Alex , Bharath, Oleg, Vamsi , Vinay . Created to empower the Canada traveller with instant information and comprehensive choices, the company began its journey in the Canada travel market. It aimed to offer a range of best-value services along with cutting-edge technology and dedicated round-the-clock customer support.

After consolidating its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency, Toronto Tourism followed its success in the Canada by launching its Canada operations in 2018. Toronto Tourism offers travellers the convenience of online travel information at finger tips. Rapidly, Toronto Tourism became the preferred choice of millions of travellers who were delighted to be empowered by a few mouse clicks!

"We provide a wealth of services – this website is just one example – so that travellers can plan a great Toronto experience tailored to their needs. Our association also works closely with meeting planners and tour operators to ensure that the city exceeds their hosts, delegates and clients expectations."
